# Next 20 Mathematical Models in Marketing Papers (21-40)

## 21. Conjoint Measurement (Green & Rao)
**Key Paper:** Green, Paul E., and V. R. Rao. (1971). "Conjoint Measurement for Quantifying Judgmental Data." *Journal of Marketing Research*.
**Description:** Foundational paper for conjoint analysis.

## 22. Decision Calculus (Little)
**Key Paper:** Little, John D. C. (1970). "Models and Managers: The Concept of a Decision Calculus." *Management Science*.
**Description:** Introduced user-friendly models for managers.

## 23. Logit Model on Scanner Data (Guadagni & Little)
**Key Paper:** Guadagni, Peter M., and John D. C. Little. (1983). "A Logit Model of Brand Choice Calibrated on Scanner Data." *Marketing Science*.
**Description:** Applied logit models to scanner data for brand choice.

## 24. Multinomial Logit Choice (Gensch & Recker)
**Key Paper:** Gensch, Dennis H., and Wilfred W. Recker. (1979). "The Multinomial, Multiattribute Logit Choice Model." *Journal of Marketing Research*.
**Description:** Multiattribute logit models for consumer choice.

## 25. Heterogeneity in Brand Preferences (Chintagunta et al.)
**Key Paper:** Chintagunta, Pradeep, Dipak Jain, and N. Vilcassim. (1991). "Investigating Heterogeneity in Brand Preferences in Logit Models for Panel Data." *Journal of Marketing Research*.
**Description:** Addressed heterogeneity in consumer preferences.

## 26. Purchase-Timing and Brand-Switching (Vilcassim & Jain)
**Key Paper:** Vilcassim, N. J., and D. C. Jain. (1991). "Modeling Purchase-Timing and Brand-Switching Behavior..." *Journal of Marketing Research*.
**Description:** Modeled timing and switching with unobserved heterogeneity.

## 27. Market Share Functions (Moorthy)
**Key Paper:** Moorthy, K. Sridhar. (1984). "Market Share Functions." *Marketing Science*.
**Description:** Theoretical modeling of market share dynamics.

## 28. Optimal Direct Mail Selection (Bult & Wansbeek)
**Key Paper:** Bult, J. R., and T. Wansbeek. (1995). "Optimal Selection for Direct Mail." *Marketing Science*.
**Description:** Quantitative models for direct marketing optimization.

## 29. Dynamic Marketing Mix (Naik et al.)
**Key Paper:** Naik, P. A., et al. (2005). "What Happens When Sales Fall..." *Journal of Marketing Research*.
**Description:** Dynamic marketing mix modeling.

## 30. New Product Development (Hauser)
**Key Paper:** Hauser, John R. (1988). "New Product Development: A Method for Generating and Testing Concepts." *Marketing Science*.
**Description:** Mathematical models for NPD and concept testing.

## 31. Market Response Models (Hanssens et al.)
**Key Paper:** Hanssens, D. M., Parsons, L. J., & Schultz, R. L. (1990). "Market Response Models: Econometric and Time Series Analysis."
**Description:** Comprehensive text on econometric market response.

## 32. Bayesian Statistics in Marketing (Allenby et al.)
**Key Paper:** Allenby, G. M., Rossi, P. E., & McCulloch, R. (2005). "Bayesian Statistics and Marketing."
**Description:** Application of Bayesian models to marketing problems.

## 33. Design and Marketing of New Products (Urban & Hauser)
**Key Paper:** Urban, G. L., & Hauser, J. R. (1980). "Design and Marketing of New Products."
**Description:** Framework integrating models for NPD.

## 34. Conjoint Analysis Review (Green & Srinivasan)
**Key Paper:** Green, P. E., & Srinivasan, V. (1978). "Conjoint Analysis in Consumer Research: Issues and Outlook."
**Description:** Comprehensive review of conjoint analysis.

## 35. Thirty Years of Conjoint (Green & Krieger)
**Key Paper:** Green, P. E., & Krieger, A. M. (2001). "Thirty Years of Conjoint Analysis: Reflections and Prospects."
**Description:** Reflection on the evolution of conjoint analysis.

## 36. Marketing Engineering (Lilien & Rangaswamy)
**Key Paper:** Lilien, G. L., & Rangaswamy, A. (2003). "Marketing Engineering."
**Description:** Practical application of quantitative models.

## 37. Marketing Models (Lilien et al.)
**Key Paper:** Lilien, G. L., Kotler, P., & Moorthy, K. S. (1992). "Marketing Models."
**Description:** Synthesis of marketing models.

## 38. Logit Model for Decision Making (Coskunoglu et al.)
**Key Paper:** Coskunoglu, O., et al. (1985). "A New Logit Model for Decision Making..."
**Description:** New logit model applications.

## 39. Diffusion of Innovations (Rogers)
**Key Paper:** Rogers, E. M. (1962). "Diffusion of Innovations."
**Description:** Foundational concepts for diffusion models.

## 40. Advanced Regression Analysis
**Key Paper:** Various Authors. "Regression Analysis in Marketing."
**Description:** Foundational statistical modeling for marketing.
