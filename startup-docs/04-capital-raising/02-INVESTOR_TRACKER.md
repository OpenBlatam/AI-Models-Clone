## Status Definitions

- **Cold**: No contact yet
- **Warm Intro**: Introduction requested/received
- **Meeting Scheduled**: Meeting on calendar
- **Pitched**: Meeting completed
- **In DD**: Due diligence started
- **Term Sheet**: Term sheet received/sent
- **Negotiating**: Terms being negotiated
- **Closed**: Investment confirmed
- **Passed**: Not interested / we passed
