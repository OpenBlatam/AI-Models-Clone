## Slide 2: The Problem

- **280 million people** worldwide struggle with substance use disorders
- Traditional recovery support is **expensive, inaccessible, and limited by availability**

**The gap:** No scalable, affordable, AI-powered solution that provides personalized, evidence-based recovery support around the clock.

- **Progress tracking** with data-driven insights
- **Community connection** with peer support networks
- **Privacy-first** approach with HIPAA-compliant infrastructure
- **Multi-modal support:** Text, voice, and video interactions
